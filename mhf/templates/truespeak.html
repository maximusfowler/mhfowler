Share Your Story
If you backup your iPhone onto a macbook, you can come out to the world as yourself by uploading your text message history to a publicly viewable page.

How To Upload Your Texts

  1. Click the download button below, to download a script which will help you upload your text messages.
  2. After the download completes, double click truespeak.zip to unzip the script.
  3. Right click truespeak (the unzipped file) and click open to run the script which will guide you through uploading your text messages to a secret page which only you have a link to.
  4. From your secret page select which of your text conversations you would like to publicly publish under which aliases.
